PeerLadder

A national peer-mentoring initiative supporting underrepresented dRTP aspiring leaders

Saskia Lawson-Tovey1, Xenia Perez-Sitja2, Sara Villa3, Carole Goble1 1University of Manchester · 2Earlham Institute · 3OLS

Inspired by ELIXIR Leadership programme – the team behind PeerLadder are ELEAD alumni, current participants and co-leads.

PeerLadder is a new peer-mentoring initiative in the UK.

  • Supporting leadership development
  • Empowering underrepresented digital Research Technical Professionals (dRTPs)
  • Building confidence, community, and inclusive leadership

Structured peer-mentoring

Open Leadership training from OLS

Psychological safety & guidance

Supportive materials and a facilitated space where it's safe to speak about real obstacles.
